Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is often called the "City of Light" for its pivotal role in the Age of Enlightenment. The Eiffel Tower, one of the most iconic structures in the world, dominates the skyline. The Louvre Museum, home to the famous *Mona Lisa*, is a cultural treasure trove. The city's streets are a blend of historic charm and modern elegance, with cafés lining boulevards like the Champs-Élysées. Paris is known for its fashion, hosting prestigious events like Paris Fashion Week. The Seine River runs through the city, offering picturesque views and romantic boat rides. Montmartre, with its bohemian vibe, was once the home of many famous artists. The city's architecture is a mix of Gothic, Renaissance, and Art Deco styles. Parisians take pride in their culinary scene, with pâtisseries, bistros, and Michelin-starred restaurants. Paris is a symbol of art, love, and culture, inspiring millions of visitors each year.
